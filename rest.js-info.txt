
Basic CRUD Kullanımı için :

    GET(all)     localhost:3000/api/v1/books
    GET(single)  localhost:3000/api/v1/books/:id
    POST         localhost:3000/api/v1/books
    PUT          localhost:3000/api/v1/books/:id
    DELETE       localhost:3000/api/v1/books/:id



Pro CRUD Kullanımı Detayları (query, select, sort, skip, limit, distinct, populate vb) için :
https://florianholzapfel.github.io/express-restify-mongoose/
